Aula de Fotografía Subterránea (Work in progress)

"Aula de Fotografía Subterránea" is a group based in Spain. They are making trips and exploring differents caves and mountains and taking photos as well. Therefore, they need also and space online to show both their activities and the photos they have taken.

So this web page is a blog that I have built from scratch. There are two differents parts in this web: the blog side, where you can see the post, send comments and more, and the managment side, where the admins can publish a post, add a new administrator and more options. I have made the whole CMS from the ground up mainly with PHP.

I'm working in this website now so don't worry if you see that any section is not operative at the moment.
